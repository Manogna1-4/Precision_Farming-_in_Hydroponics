Smart Irrigation System in Precision Farming and Hydroponics

Our project, HydroGrow, is a precision farming solution using hydroponics and IoT to optimize crop growth. By monitoring and controlling water flow, temperature, and nutrient delivery, we ensure plants receive only what’s needed, reducing waste and conserving resources.

About the Project

Precision Hydroponic Farming

To achieve precision farming in hydroponics—maximizing crop yield while minimizing water and nutrient use—you need an integrated, sensor-driven, and automated system that precisely controls all key growth parameters.

1. Water Management

Precision hydroponic systems implement recirculating systems such as NFT, DWC, and ebb and flow, allowing reuse of water and reducing consumption by up to 90%. Drip irrigation delivers water directly to the root zones, cutting evaporation losses. Moisture sensors enable automated irrigation by tracking real-time root zone conditions.

2. Nutrient Management

Nutrient delivery is optimized through automated dosing based on real-time pH and EC sensor feedback. pH sensors automate the addition of pH-up or pH-down solutions, while EC sensors help in adjusting nutrient concentration. Integration with cloud platforms allows remote control, monitoring, and instant alerts for anomalies.

3. Water Flow & Temperature Control

Water is circulated using adjustable, energy-efficient pumps tailored to system needs. Temperature sensors help manage both ambient and root zone conditions using HVAC systems, chillers, or heaters. Evaporation is reduced using covered channels, greenhouses, or mulch.

4. System Design Example

A typical setup includes a water reservoir storing nutrient solution, submersible or DC pumps for flow regulation, and sensors for pH, EC, and temperature. Moisture sensors track root zone saturation, while a microcontroller (like Arduino) manages automation. Nutrients are delivered through drip, NFT, or DWC systems with cloud-based apps for remote control and alerts.

5. Outcomes

The system reduces water usage by 50–90%, improves nutrient efficiency through precise dosing, and boosts crop yield with optimal environmental control. This sustainable model also offers long-term cost savings.

Summary

A precision hydroponic system leverages automation, recirculated water, and targeted climate control to deliver the exact requirements of plants, ensuring optimal growth, minimal waste, and high sustainability.
